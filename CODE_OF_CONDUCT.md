# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Welcome diverse perspectives
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1, available at https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
